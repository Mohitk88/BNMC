ABOUT US

Establishment

BNM College, Barhiya was established in the year 1959, BNM College, Barhiya is affiliated to Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University, Bhagalpur BadriNarayan Mukteshwar College, Barhiya is the oldest institute of higher learning in the District Lakhisarai. Late Badri Narayan Mukteshwar has a dream to open a higher learning institute for the students of rural area. B.N.M College, Barhiya believe in providing quality education and information about courses to students& their parents to matching their needs in collaboration of Search Education Portal.

The College ensures publicity and transparency in the admission process.It is very particular to cuter to the needs of differently abled students.The college is primitives to gender sensitivity,inclusive education and environment friendly ethos among students. The Purpose of the B.N.M.College, Barhiya is to stimulate and coordinate the institutional resources of the College and to challenge faculties and students

The chief objective of the college since its inception has been to shape the young minds with the urge for creativity spirit of tolerance and scientific temper.To cope with changing need of the society and economy.

Affiliation

Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University, Bhagalpur has 42 affiliated Colleges In the beginning, college was affiliated to Bihar University and later to TilkaManjhi Bhagalpur University.It the beginning time college have only Arts Faculty and in 1968 college started science faculty with Biology.

Late GopeshwarPd Singh was the first secretary of B.N.M College and Late Mahesh Pd Singh,Ex. Agriculture Minister Bihar Govt. was first chair person of the college.Late on LalaBabu and a few other social works was in governing body of the college.After crossing several hurdles,they achieved the goal and made their dream true by establishing a college in rural area.In 1962 college was shifted in his own building. New building was situated on the bank of Ganga River. Now B.N.M. College Barhiya is running in 4 Acre compound with 2500 SqMtr Built Area.
